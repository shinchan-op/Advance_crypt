FAQ

Common questions

Here are some of the most common questions that we get.
What is encryption? Encryption is the process of converting plain text into a secret code to protect sensitive information.
What is decryption? Decryption is the process of converting encoded or encrypted data back into its original form.
Why is encryption important for cybersecurity? Encryption helps protect data from unauthorized access, ensuring confidentiality and privacy in digital communications.
What are some common encryption algorithms? Common encryption algorithms include AES, RSA, DES, and Blowfish.
How can I ensure the security of my encrypted messages? To ensure security, use strong encryption algorithms, keep encryption keys secure, and regularly update encryption protocols.
